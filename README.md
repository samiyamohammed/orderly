# 🛍️ Orderly

An offline-first mobile app for managing and organizing product sales, inventory, and daily business activities.

---

## ✨ About

**Orderly** is a simple and efficient tool designed for small businesses, sellers, and individuals who want to manage their products and sales without relying on internet connectivity.

It helps you:

* Track products and inventory
* Record daily sales
* Monitor business activity
* Stay organized — anytime, anywhere

All features work **fully offline**, making it fast, reliable, and accessible even with limited connectivity.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📦 Product Management

* Add, edit, and delete products
* Track price, quantity, and details

### 💰 Sales Tracking

* Record sales transactions
* View daily or total sales
* Simple and quick entry system

### 📊 Inventory Overview

* Monitor stock levels
* Avoid running out of products

### ⚡ Offline-First

* Works without internet
* Instant performance and fast loading

### 🔒 Private & Secure

* All data stored locally on your device
* No external servers required
